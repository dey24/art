ABOUT US

TARANG- Connecting Artist

TARANG is an initiative in respect of artist where artist can join from all over the world. It was created by two friends Sahil Sharma and Sarthak Sharma and implemented the concept on 16th April 2020.
The motivation behind it to create this community is just to connect the artists from all over India in the time of this pandemic. Recently we have connected to 300+ artists from all over India and few are from different parts of world.
